Hi, I'm Nathalie!

So, you’re making games all day? That’s what people assume when I tell them what I study. But Creative Media and Game Technologies is much more than that, it gives me the opportunity to connect my two favorite things; creativity and technology. With my specialization as designer I learn a broad set of skills which range from 3D design till HTML/CSS. I enjoy working in a structured manner and I’m familiar with scrum to keep track of progress. I’m curious and I love to learn new skills every day. I want to inspire other people to keep growing, by using my designer skills to enhance the way people learn. That’s why I look forward to work on serious games or E-learning materials in the future.
